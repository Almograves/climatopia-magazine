  • Thermal expansion:
    Seawater expands due to increasing temperatures
  • Melting glaciers:
    Summer runoff isn't balanced by winter snowfall anymore
  • Melting ice shields:
    The ice shields in Greenland and Antarctica are melting

This trend is increasing, with the yearly rise in sea-level accelerating.
